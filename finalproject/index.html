Turn Back Tornadoes

June 18, 2020
Written by John Johnston

In a press conference today the President of the weather committee stated that we should fight back against the tornadoes. Several ideas were proposed such as bombing tornadoes, create a large vacuum to remove the air from the tornado, but the most time was spent talking about flying planes around a tornado in the opposite direction.

The President has decided to put resources into this effort in order to reduce the damage done to communities across the United States. The plan is to send two planes circling around a tornado, flying in the opposite direction. The purpose of this is to attempt to slow down the rotation of the tornado and hopefully reduce or eliminate the damage done by the tornado.
