Welcome to ~Scoots

~Scoots is a motor scooter rental company located on the island of Cozumel off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea. We cater to locals, walk-in tourists, enthusiasts, and cruise-based tourists looking for a little adventure. We have friendly staff and easy to rent services of motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-sides ATVs.
