El Cielo Cozumel "Sky/Heaven"

El Cielo Cozumel, known as "HEAVEN", is one of Cozumel's best kept secrets. It is the most magnificent place in the Caribbean Sea. Cozumel's El Cielo is only accessible by boat and is about a mile from Cozumel's Shoreline, and a 25 minute boat ride from the nearest cruise pier. El Cielo has shallow crystal clear water as far as the eye can see, which makes it it a perfect place to relax and enjoy the Caribbean Sea. El Cielo Cozumel is covered with soft powdered white sand that massage your feet every step you take. There are lots of starfish located in El Cielo and you'll as find stingrays gliding by only yards from you, also enjoying El Cielo (Stingrays are harmless).

Punta Sur Cozumel

Punta Sur Park in Cozumel, is at the southern tip of Cozumel Island, and offers an amazing exploration of Cozumel’s most spectacular ecological reservation. You will visit one of the worlds most amazing scenic views from a top the Celarain lighthouse, with a nautical museum at its base. There is the Laguna de Colombia, part of a three-lagoon system that is the habitat of crocodiles. Crocs can be seen from either the shore, from a trail through mangroves or from the dock over the lagoon. You may climb some stairs to a nice lookout over the lagoon, and there is also a trail through mangroves. There is a boat ride that takes people to discover the lagoon a couple times a day. You may see many resident and migratory waterfowl and many other rare & unique creatures during your exciting exploration of Punta Sur Cozumel. Its a real treat to see the different animals at the ecological reserve at Punta Sur. You can see anything from island Raccoons (much smaller and friendlier than normal Raccoons and its also a rare type of Raccoon only found in Cozumel) and even flamingos.

San Gervasio Ruins

This Mayan complex is Cozumel’s preserved ruins’. San Gervasio is thought to have been the site of the sanctuary of Ixchel, goddess of fertility, and thus an important pilgrimage site at which Maya women worshiped. If your looking to experience the mystic San Gervasio Mayan Ruins, our Custom Cozumel Jeep tour is perfect. You may customize the Cozumel Jeep tour as you please and visit the best Mayan ruins in Cozumel, including the hidden Mayan ruins not accessible by car and only with 4x4 vehicles, like our jeeps.

Other Side of Cozumel

The Other Side of Cozumel (East side of Cozumel & also known as the "Wild Side") has 7 miles of secluded ocean & beach front that is absolutely breath-taking. If your looking to get the most out of Cozumel in a single day, the other side of Cozumel (East Side) is the perfect place to enjoy gorgeous deserted beaches, off-road jungle terrain, blow holes, hidden water caves, beach restaurants, hatching baby sea turtles, snorkeling, rock formations and much more. The best Eco Reserves in Cozumel, is the famous Punta Sur Park. At Punta Sur Cozumel, you'll discover a lighthouse, named the "Celarain Lighthouse", where you may capture the most amazing sights.
