A Chance of Sun

Domain Name: achanceofsun.com

Site Name:

The name of the site is A Chance of Sun. Meteorologists are often known for making weather predicitions that turn out to be incorrect. So by always forecasting a "Chance" of sun you will never be wrong.

Site Purpose:

The purpose of the site is to provide accurate weather forecasts for Eastern Idaho. The intent is to provide weather forecasts that will never be inaccurate. So the site will provide the expected forecast, but also include that there is a "Chance of Sun". Whether or not there is sun that day, is irrelavant because we only forecasted a chance of sun. The site will also include satire peices about weather to keep the visitors entertained and coming back.

Site Logo:

The logo is a cloud with the sun peaking out. I think this is a good representation of "A Chance of Sun".
