Target Audience:

The site is intended for those age 14 to 60 that are intelligent, but also looking for humor in life. They will visit the site for the most accurate weather report because at this site we are never wrong in forecasting a chance of sun. The weather satire in combination with accurate weather forecasts is what will drive the audience to regularly return to the site.

Scenarios:

  • A parent has their 12yr old daughter's 6th grade graduation on Friday and it is outdoors, is it going to rain?
  • Saturday I am going to the airshow, will I need to bring sun protection?
  • The forecast has been dreary for 2 weeks, is there any chance of sun within the next week?
  • A Roofer needs to repair a customer's roof tomorrow and wants to make sure there isn't rain in the forecast.
  • Millenial going out tonight, but needs a laugh and the satirical article about stopping tornados by flying planes in circles in the opposite direction does the job.
